Design Focused Maps

These are 2 maps created to show polished maps for presentation or documentation rather than interactivity. Both were first created in ArcPRO then ported to Adobe Illustrator to modify symbology and add features unavailable in ArcPRO.

Political Map of African Countries

This map of Africa showcases methods for organizing and attributing labels and symbols by hierarchy and landform in information dense layouts.




This thematic map, something you might seen in a magazine or brochure, doesn't use features normally included in professional maps like a north arrow or scale, but rather is structured to quickly convey information about certain areas to the reader with images and text.
